Who We Are

It was founded in 1945 by HRS. GROUP COMPANY is a company in the field of legally legalized law firms that are very prominent nationally and internationally. Our practice includes criminal and civil litigation, arbitration, bankruptcy, state administrative law, and dispute resolution issues.

We are pleased to share that we have been trusted to become the counsel of Mr. Joko Widodo, Indonesia’s 9th President, and his family in various civil and administrative matters. We were also trusted to represent Mr. Prabowo Subianto and Mr. Gibran Rakabuming Raka, Indonesia’s President-Elect and Vice President Elect for the 2024-2029 term, in the presidential election dispute at the Constitutional Court.

OHA is committed to deliver the highest standards of service and we believe that our deep knowledge, experience, and dedication has synergize to deliver the best service to clients. With over three decades of experience, our team consist of highly experienced attorneys that haverepresented some of the most high-profile domestic and international clients, from high net-worth individuals, state-owned enterprises, to multinational companies in various complex dispute resolution matters.

As a highly regarded litigation law firms, our experiences have enabled us to cultivate a valuable working relationship with government agencies, regulators and key industry player that further benefited our clients for future collaboration. Throughout our journey, we have also built a solid working relationship with a reputable international law firms that allows us to provide seamless service for cross border disputes and transactions.

As the Founding Partner, Otto has been an active member of the International Bar Association since 1985. Domestically, he is also a notable organizational figure. Otto was the president of the Indonesian Bar Association (IKADIN) for two terms in 2003-2015 and is currently the president of the Indonesian Advocates Association (PERADI). In the public sector, Otto was a member of the Indonesian Penal Code Drafters and was also appointed as the Chief Legal Advisor of Indonesia’s Coordinating Minister for Maritime affairs.
